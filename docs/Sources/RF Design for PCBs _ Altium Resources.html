Learning Hub RF Design

RF / Wireless Design for PCBs

Radio frequency (RF) designs operate at high frequencies, and they require careful layout and routing to prevent signal integrity problems. An RF PCB layout can sometimes contain a section with digital components, and careful layout can help prevent interference between RF and digital signals. Browse our library of resources to learn more about RF PCB design, layout, routing, and signal integrity.
